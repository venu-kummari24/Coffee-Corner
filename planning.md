# Coffee Corner - Project Plan

## Project Overview

Coffee Corner is a modern local cafe that serves fresh coffee, tea, breakfast, snacks, desserts, and refreshing beverages.
The cafe provides a warm, clean, and comfortable environment where customers can relax, meet friends, work remotely, or spend quality time with family.

## Business Information

Business Name : Coffee Corner

Business Type : Local Cafe

Location : Hyderabad, India 

Working Hours : Monday to Sunday
Time : 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM

## Mission

To serve fresh coffee and delicious food while providing excellent customer service in a comfortable and friendly environment.


## Vision

To become the favorite neighborhood cafe by offering high-quality food, fresh coffee, and memorable customer experiences.

## Target Audience

Our cafe is designed for:

- College Students
- Office Employees
- Families
- Friends
- Couples
- Freelancers
- Coffee Lovers

## Customer Needs

Customers usually look for:

- Fresh coffee
- Delicious food
- Affordable prices
- Comfortable seating
- Free Wi-Fi
- Fast service
- Clean environment
- Friendly staff

## Our Solution

Coffee Corner provides:

- Freshly brewed coffee
- Premium tea
- Fresh breakfast
- Delicious snacks
- Homemade desserts
- Comfortable seating
- Free Wi-Fi
- Friendly customer service
- Hygienic environment

## Services

### Coffee

Freshly brewed coffee made from premium coffee beans.

### Tea

Traditional and herbal tea served hot and fresh.

### Breakfast

Healthy breakfast including sandwiches, toast, and pancakes.

### Snacks

French fries, burgers, pizza, wraps, and garlic bread.

### Desserts

Fresh cakes, brownies, muffins, and pastries.

### Cold Beverages

Milkshakes, smoothies, fresh juice, and cold coffee.

## Unique Selling Proposition

Fresh Coffee, Fresh Food, Happy Moments.
