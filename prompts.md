# AI Prompts - Coffee Corner Website

This document contains all the prompts used to generate the website content for Coffee Corner.

## Prompt 1 - Homepage Content

Write a professional homepage copy for a modern local cafe named "Coffee Corner".

Include:

- Hero Heading
- Hero Subheading
- Welcome Message
- Why Choose Us
- Featured Services
- Call-to-Action

Use a friendly, modern, and customer-focused tone.

Keep the language simple and attractive.

### Purpose

This prompt is designed to generate an attractive homepage that creates a positive first impression and encourages customers to visit the cafe.

## Prompt 2 - About Us

Write an About Us section for Coffee Corner.

Explain:

- Our Story
- Our Mission
- Our Vision
- Our Commitment to Quality

Use simple English and a friendly tone.

This prompt creates trust by introducing the cafe and its values.

## Prompt 3 - Menu Content

Write a menu section for Coffee Corner.

Include:

- Coffee
- Tea
- Breakfast
- Snacks
- Desserts
- Cold Beverages

Give a short and attractive description for each category.

This prompt generates clear descriptions of the cafe's food and drink categories.

## Prompt 4 - Why Choose Coffee Corner

Write six reasons why customers should choose Coffee Corner.

Use short, attractive, and customer-friendly sentences.

## Prompt 5 - Customer Reviews

Write three realistic customer testimonials for Coffee Corner.

Each review should be positive, friendly, and believable.

This prompt creates social proof and builds trust.

## Prompt 6 - Contact Section

Write a Contact Us section for Coffee Corner.

Include:

- Friendly introduction
- Phone Number
- Email
- Address
- Opening Hours
- Invitation to visit the cafe

This prompt generates a professional contact section that makes it easy for customers to reach the cafe.

## Prompt 7 - CTA

Write three powerful Call-to-Action messages for Coffee Corner.

The CTA should encourage customers to:

- Visit the cafe
- Reserve a table
- Try the menu

Keep each CTA short and persuasive.

This prompt encourages customers to take action after reading the website content.

## Prompt Engineering Summary

The prompts were carefully designed to generate clear, engaging, and customer-focused website content. Each prompt focuses on a specific website section, making the AI output more accurate, organized, and suitable for a professional cafe website.
